Who we are

DreamAliveCenter COVID – 19: SAVE LIVES NOW

The second wave of COVID – 19 has been devastating on lives and livelihoods, and as of June 18, 2021, India continues to have more than 600,000 cases reported in a day. Health authorities have also been warning of a possible third wave of infections that may affect peri-urban and rural settlements. There is a dire need for hospital beds, doctors, medical equipment and consumables, and greater awareness around COVID appropriate behaviour in communities. DreamAliveCenter India with its 70 years of experience in providing relief during disasters and delivering large-scale health programmes on the ground is well-positioned to support the affected citizens where the need is the greatest. We are part of the CARE International Confederation working in over 100 countries for a world where all people live with dignity and security.

"DreamAliveCenter is reducing the burden on the health system with make-shift dedicated COVID health centres, PPE kits for frontline workers, and raising awareness about COVID-19 appropriate behaviour."
